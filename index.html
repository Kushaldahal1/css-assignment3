Interesting Facts

Rich caramelized flavor and a delicious combination of sweet and savory flavors characterize butter scotch ice cream. Its particular, buttery twist is from the toffee or caramel that is blended into the foundation, giving it its distinct flavor. It's interesting to note that this flavor gained popularity in the 20th century and has since spread throughout the world's ice cream shops.
